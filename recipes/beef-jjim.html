갈비찜 Galbi-jjim

  • Ingredients:
  • 6 lbs beef short ribs,
  • 1/2 onion,
  • soy sauce,
  • sugar,
  • 2 carrots,
  • 2 potatoes
  1. Cover beef in large bowl with water. Leave to drain blood, about 3 hours.
  2. Boil meat in fresh water--scum will rise from the meat. Drain the water.
  3. Pour enough water to cover meat in slow cooker. Slice onions and put inside. Add soy sauce and sugar to taste.
  4. Boil until meat is cooked and tender, about 1.5 hours. Regularly skim the surface to remove scum.
  5. Remove broth to heat-safe bowl and let cool overnight in refrigerator. Dispose solidified fat in trashcan.
  6. Pour broth back into slow cooker with meat. Chop potatoes and carrots into bite-sized pieces. Add to pot.
  7. Boil until potatoes are soft and liquid has almost disappeared.
