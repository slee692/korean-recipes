갈비찜 Galbi-jjim

  • Ingredients:
  • 6 lbs beef short ribs
  • 2 carrots
  • 2 potatoes
  1. Place beef in large bowl and pour enough water to just cover the meat. Leave to drain blood, about 3 hours.
  2. Drain water and place meat in slow cooker. Pour water to cover again. Heat until boiling--this will give rise to little pieces and unwanted fat from the meat. Drain the water.
  3. Pour enough water to cover meat. Boil until meat is cooked and tender, about ___ hours. Regularly skim the surface to remove scum.
