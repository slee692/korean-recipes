잡채 Japchae

  • Ingredients:
  • 1 bundle glass noodles,
  • 1 tbsp minced garlic,
  • 1/2 cup spinach,
  • 1 carrot,
  • 1/2 onion,
  • chives,
  • 3 sticks imitation crab,
  • 1 tbsp sesame seed oil,
  • sesame seeds,
  • soy sauce
  1. Boil spinach in water for 2-3 minutes. Drain and combine in large bowl with minced garlic.
  2. Boil water on high and thoroughly cook noodles.
  3. Julienne carrot and onion. Put in frying pan with olive oil and cook until carrot is soft. Add into bowl.
  4. Cut chives into inch-long pieces. Rip imitation crab. Add into bowl with noodles.
  5. Add sesame seeds, sesame seed oil, and soy sauce to taste.
