생선전 Fish Jeon

  • Ingredients:
  • 3 fillets tilapia,
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour,
  • 4 sticks imitation crab,
  • 4 eggs
  1. Cut tilapia into thin, bite-sized pieces.
  2. Cover fish in flour.
  3. Mince imitation crab and mix with eggs. Cover fish with mixture.
  4. Cook in frying pan over low-medium heat.
