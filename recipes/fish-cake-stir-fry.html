어묵볶음 Fish Cake Stir Fry

  • Ingredients:
  • 1/2 pack fish cake,
  • 2 potatoes,
  • 2 tbsp minced garlic
  • soy sauce,
  • 3 chopped green onions
  1. Briefly submerge fish cake in boiling water to remove oil, about 5 minutes.
  2. Chop fish cake and potatoes into same sized rectangles.
  3. Add minced garlic to pot and heat on high for 2-3 minutes. Add potatoes, green onions, and enough soy sauce to cover.
  4. Once potatoes soften, add fish cake. Cook at low heat until soy sauce disappears. Toss fish cake to thoroughly cover in sauce.
  5. Once mixture does not fall from spatula (about 10 min), remove from heat. Pour into rectangular box as a mold and let it cool down.
