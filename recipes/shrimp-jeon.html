새우전 Shrimp Jeon

  • Ingredients:
  • 2 lbs Raw Shrimp (peeled and deveined),
  • 5 baby carrots or 1 carrot,
  • 3 green onions,
  • 3 eggs,
  • all-purpose flour
  1. Brunoise the carrots into tiny pieces.--they must be small enough to soften when cooking, but big enough to provide a pop of color.
  2. Chop the green onions and throw away the roots.
  3. Cut shrimp into bite-sized pieces.
  4. Put carrots, green onions, and shrimp in large bowl. Pour just enough flour for the ingredients to begin sticking to one another, about 2 spoonfuls.
  5. Thoroughly mix in eggs.
  6. Heat up pan and place 1 large spoon of mixture at a time. Cook over low heat until both sides are golden-brown, about 15 minutes.
