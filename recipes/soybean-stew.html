된장찌개 Doenjang Jjigae

  • Ingredients:
  • 2 spoon ferment soybean paste,
  • water,
  • 3 dried anchovies
  • 1/2 tofu
  • 1/2 onion,
  • 4 potatoes,
  • 2 white mushrooms
  1. Dice onions, potatoes, tofu, and mushrooms.
  2. Pour a 1/2 pot of water. Add anchovies and boil on high heat for 5 minutes. Reduce to medium heat. Add soybean paste and stir until chunks disappear.
  3. Add potatoes, onion, and mushrooms. Once potatoes are cooked, add tofu.
  4. Continue boiling for 2 minutes and serve.
