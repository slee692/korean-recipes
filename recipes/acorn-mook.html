도토리묵 Acorn Mook

  • Ingredients:
  • 1 cup acorn powder,
  • 6 1/2 cup water,
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce,
  • 3 green onions,
  • sesame seeds
  1. Mix acorn powder and water in stove pot.
  2. Heat on high while continuously stirring, making sure to scrap the bottom and sides--you should start to see chunks of jelly.
  3. Once it begins bubbling, reduce to low heat. Add olive oil. Continue cooking at low heat and stirring until mixture thickens.
  4. Once mixture does not fall from spatula (about 10 min), remove from heat. Pour into rectangular box as a mold and let it cool down.
  5. To make the sauce, mix soy sauce, green onions, and sesame seeds.
  6. Slice cool mook into rectangles and top with sauce.
